Common signs of wildlife intrusion

Scratching, thumping, or squeaking sounds in walls, attic, or crawl spaces, especially at night

Droppings, nesting materials, or chewed wires in attics, walls, or crawl spaces

Damaged soffits, vents, roof shingles, or gaps in siding where animals have entered

Strong, musky odors from droppings, urine, or dead animals in or around your property

Wildlife seen on your property, near trash cans, or entering/exiting your home

Torn insulation, damaged ductwork, or property damage from animal activity

Why professional wildlife removal matters

Health & Safety

Wildlife can carry diseases like rabies, hantavirus, and roundworm. Droppings and dead animals create serious health hazards.

Property Damage

Animals can tear apart insulation, damage wiring, crush ductwork, and create costly structural problems.

Prevent Return

Without proper exclusion, wildlife will return to the same entry points, causing ongoing damage and problems.

Fire Risk

Chewed electrical wires from animal activity can create serious fire hazards in your home.

Peace of Mind

Professional removal plus exclusion keeps your home sealed, clean, and protected from future wildlife entry.
